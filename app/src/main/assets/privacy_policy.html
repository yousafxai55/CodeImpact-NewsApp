This Privacy Policy explains how NewsApp (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our Android application (the “App”). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. What the App Does

NewsApp fetches public news content via RSS/Atom feeds from third-party publishers and displays it in the App. We do not host or control the original content and links; they belong to the respective publishers.

2. Information We Collect

  • No account / No signup: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal details to use the App.
  • On-device data only: Your bookmarks and saved searches are stored locally on your device using an on-device database (Room). We do not upload this data to our servers.
  • Network requests: The App makes requests to news publishers’ websites to retrieve public RSS/feeds and images. Those third-party sites may collect their own technical information per their policies.

3. Analytics & Advertising

By default, the App does not integrate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. If we introduce analytics or ads in a future update, we will update this Policy and, where required, ask for your consent inside the App.

4. Permissions

The App primarily uses internet access to load news content and images. If additional permissions are requested in future versions, we will explain why and how they are used.

5. Third-Party Websites & Content

Links and content shown in the App may open third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content. Please review the privacy policy of any site you visit.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable measures to keep your on-device data secure. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Effective Date” and, where required, by notifying you in the App.
